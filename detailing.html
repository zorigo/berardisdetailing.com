Auto Detailing


Berardi's Detailing has been servicing over 6,000 customers in the Tri-State area. We offer a personalized and convenient detailing service that comes with your satisfaction guaranteed. Cars are done at our location or yours. Berardi's Detailing has both interior and exterior detailing services available, and full detailing. Berardi's Details all makes and models of automobiles, trucks, vans, RV's, and boats. We have the skills, tools, experience to make any car or truck look like it rolled off the showroom floor. Berardi's uses only the finest products on your vehicle available. We offer free pick up and delivery at your home or office. Berardi's also has rental cars available. Throughout our many years of detailing we have been honored by many car dealerships, car clubs, business's, personal clients as the BEST detailing company in the Tri State Area. We take great pride in our work and we continue to provide outstanding detailing work to you.




Pricing

Spiff - From $25 - $45
A complete exterior hand car wash with removal of bugs and tar, then we clean the wheels and wheel wells and dry the exterior of your vehicle. Tire dressing is applied to the tires. Interior of vehicle is vacuumed. Then we clean all the glass on the interior and exterior.

Wash, Wax and Vac - From $75 - $120
A full exterior hand car wash including, wheels, tires, and wheel wells. Bugs and tar are then removed. Next we clay bar the all paint surfaces of vehicle. Then we apply 100% carnauba wax to the entire exterior of your vehicle. Tires are dressed for a high luster, and the interior of the car is vacuumed. Windows are cleaned and left spotless.

Exterior Detail - From $100 -$150
Full exterior hand car wash, including complete cleaning of wheels, tires, and wheel wells. Then we remove all bugs and tar. Clay bar treatment to exterior of vehicle. Then we compound any scratches off the exterior of the paint and polish exterior of car. Then we apply 100% carnauba wax and remove it by hand. All chrome is polished and the rubber is cleaned. tires are dressed for a high luster. All windows are cleaned on the exterior of car.

Interior Detail - From $90 - $150
Interior is vacuumed including the trunk. Then we remove all floor mats. Next we shampoo all the carpeting, upholstery, clean the door panels, compartments, headliner, ashtrays, instruments, knobs, and wood trim. Leather is cleaned and conditioned (if applies). Air vents are Q-Tipped and the dashboard is cleaned. All fabrics are protected with scotch guard protection. All windows are cleaned on the inside and left spotless.

Full Detail - From $175 - $295
Full exterior hand car wash including complete cleaning of wheels, tires, wheel wells, doorjambs. Then we remove all bugs, tar, and tree sap. Next we clay bar the entire exterior of your car. Then we compound any scratches off the exterior of the car. From there we polish the whole exterior of the car. After that we apply 100% carnauba wax to the entire exterior of the car and remove it by hand. Chrome, grill, tailpipes, and emblems are all polished. Interior is vacuumed. Then we remove floor mats and shampoo all the carpeting, and upholstery, clean door panels, compartments, headliner, ashtrays, instruments, knobs, and wood trim. Leather is cleaned and conditioned (if applies). Tires are dressed for a high luster. the air vents are Q-Tipped and the dashboard is cleaned. All fabrics are protected with a scotch guard protection. All widows and vanity mirrors are left spotless. Only the finest products are used on your car.

Engine Detail - $100
First we degrease the entire engine bay with a strong cleaner. Then we steam clean the entire engine bay leaving a showroom new look.

Leather Cleaning and Conditioning - $60
First we clean all the leather then condition it with a special treatment.
